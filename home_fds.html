    About Hole in the 6ix

    Hole in the 6ix is review forum covering restaurants in Toronto GTA. It’s a highly-regarded restaurant review site where anyone can post a review.

    How does Hole in the 6ix come up with its own ratings?

    Your customers can visit the site to vote in a featured survey. They are asked to rate restaurants on food, décor and service on a 0-3 scale. They are asked to provide comments as well. The ratings are averaged and presented on a 30-point scale.The Hole in the 6ix editors also post restaurant reviews that include a mix of comments from user reviews.
